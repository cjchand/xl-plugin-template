<h3 id='POCSummary-POSTPOCFollowup'><strong>POST POC Followup</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Problems Encountered</strong></p>
<p>Any issues encountered during the POC, whether it be with a technology or process.</p>
<p><strong>Customizations</strong></p>
<p>Append any changes you've made to synthetic.xml for plugin extensions or type-modifications.   This is helpful to other consultants.</p>
<p><strong>Missing Functionality</strong></p>
<p>Any functionality that was missing in deployit for the POC.   This is helpful to provide to developers for consideration for future enhancements.</p>
<p><strong>Customer Feedback</strong></p>
<p>How the customer reacted to the POC during, at the end, or afterwards.   This is often used to gauge the success of the POC and determines how it will move forward in the sales process.</p>
<p><strong>Open Issues</strong></p>
